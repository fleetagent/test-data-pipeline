# Programming Jokes

1. Why do programmers prefer dark mode? Because light attracts bugs!

2. A SQL query walks into a bar, walks up to two tables and asks, "Can I join you?"

3. How many programmers does it take to change a light bulb? None. It's a hardware problem.

4. Why do Java developers wear glasses? Because they can't C#.

5. What's a programmer's favorite hangout place? Foo Bar.

6. Why did the programmer quit his job? Because he didn't get arrays.

7. There are 10 types of people in the world: those who understand binary, and those who don't.

8. A programmer's wife tells him: "Run to the store and pick up a loaf of bread. If they have eggs, get a dozen." The programmer comes home with 12 loaves of bread.

9. Why do programmers always mix up Halloween and Christmas? Because Oct 31 equals Dec 25.

10. What's the object-oriented way to become wealthy? Inheritance.
